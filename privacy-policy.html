Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Auren (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications (“Apps”).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not require users to create accounts or directly provide personal information such as name, email address, or phone number, unless explicitly stated within a specific app.

When you use our Apps, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system, language)

This information is used to improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.

2. Advertising

Our Apps may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob.

These ad providers may collect and use information to show personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your consent and regional regulations.

3. User Consent (UMP & GDPR/CCPA)

For users in regions where required (such as the EU, UK, or California), we use a User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent for:

  • Personalized advertising
  • Analytics and measurement
  • Data storage and access on the device

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time through the app’s consent settings (if available) or your device settings.

4. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand how users interact with our Apps.

Analytics data may include:

  • App launches
  • Feature usage
  • Crash reports
  • Performance metrics

This data is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form where possible.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers only for the following purposes:

  • Advertising services
  • Analytics and app performance monitoring
  • Legal compliance

All third parties are required to handle data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
